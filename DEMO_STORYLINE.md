## Demo Script for Presenter

### Opening (30 seconds)
> "Meet Nova and Mila. Mila is starting school soon, and her toddler bedroom needs a complete transformation. Watch how OTTO's Interior Designer helps them create the perfect study and sleep space in just a few minutes."

### Act 1 (1 minute)
> "Nova opens OTTO and describes the project by voice. Notice how OTTO immediately detects this is a bedroom redesign - not just decoration - and addresses Mila directly with an encouraging message."

### Act 2 (2 minutes)
> "OTTO now asks targeted questions to understand the scope:
> - First, confirm it's a full redesign with new furniture
> - Second, identify Mila's age for appropriate recommendations
> - Third, understand constraints - Nova wants to keep the cube shelf
>
> This structured consultation ensures we recommend the right products without overwhelming the family with too many questions."

### Act 3 (1.5 minutes)
> "Time for style discovery! Nova and Mila explore visual options together. They choose Modern and Scandinavian styles, then select a blue and white color palette. Notice how OTTO validates their choices and explains why they work well together."

### Act 4 (2 minutes)
> "Behind the scenes, OTTO is now:
> - Filtering 23 furniture products by age-appropriateness
> - Matching style tags across 150+ products
> - Coordinating colors with the blue and white palette
> - Balancing furniture and decor for a complete room
>
> The result? A curated moodboard with 4 furniture pieces and 3 decor items - everything needed for Mila's room transformation."

### Act 5 (1 minute)
> "Nova adds her favorites to the cart. Notice how OTTO suggests complementary items without being pushy. The family can save the moodboard and return later to add the wardrobe or rug when they're ready."

### Closing (30 seconds)
> "In under 7 minutes, OTTO helped Nova and Mila design a complete room transformation - with age-appropriate furniture, style coordination, and constraint awareness. This is the power of conversational AI combined with smart product curation."
